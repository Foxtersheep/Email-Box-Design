To pay homage to the Father of the Nation, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman and other martyrs of 15th August, North South University is going to have a flag raising ceremony on Sunday, 15th August 2021 at 12:01 AM. All Deans and Directors are expected to attend the flag raising ceremony.

Furthermore, we will be offering flowers to the portrait of Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman on the same day at 8 AM to pay tribute to the Father of the Nation. Staff and students are encouraged to join the floral wreath-laying ceremony.

Everyone is expected to wear masks and maintain social distance. 


Professor Atiqul Islam
Vice-Chancellor
North South University
